This is copied directly from another site, but I might change it someday

-   1/4c peanut butter
-   1/4c water
-   1/4c soy sauce
-   2 tbsp lime juice
-   2 cloves garlic
-   2 tbsp rice vinegar

Combine over low heat, and mix until smooth.
