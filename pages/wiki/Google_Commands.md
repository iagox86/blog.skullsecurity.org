## Tips

-   Using Google to find what type of technologies a company is using:

    site:&lt;companydomain&gt; careers
    site:&lt;companydomain&gt; jobs
    site:&lt;companydomain&gt; openings

-   Useful directives

    site:&lt;site&gt;
    link:&lt;site&gt;
    related:&lt;site&gt;
    intitle:&lt;text&gt;
    inurl:&lt;text&gt;
    filetype:&lt;type&gt;
    '.' = wildcard
    '..' = range ('2007..2009')
    '~' = synonyms
