-   2 cups water (or use pineapple juice)
-   1 cup brown sugar (packed)
-   1/2 cup white vinegar
-   2 tbsp soya sauce
-   1/2 cup ketchup
-   3 tbsp corn starch
-   2 tsp dry mustard
